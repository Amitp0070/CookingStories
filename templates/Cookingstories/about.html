I Love Food!

In this space, I am always sharing fresh, flavorful, (mostly) healthy recipes that I love to make and eat in my real, actual, everyday life. If it wouldn't eat it in real life, I won't put it on the blog. My goal is to inspire you with food that is both approachable AND exciting, whether you're cooking for yourself, your family, your roommates, or your friends. I want you to be so excited about these recipes that you eagerly await 5pm when you go home from work and start cooking.

On a related note, I absolutely LOVE seeing the food that you're making. It would make my day if you tag @girlndog on your Instagram photos and stories! We love to shout out our favorites on Fridays with our Reader Awards on Instagram Stories.

Going Deeper

Along with all the recipes, I also use the blog to share bits and pieces of my life outside the kitchen - from photos of my favorite trips, to reflections on grief and loss, to life as a dog mom.

  • Travel: From living in the Philippines, to the ultimate European adventure, to tacos in Austin! Oh my gosh, I love to travel. Check it all out here.
  • Loss: New Year's Day 2017, we lost our baby Afton the day after he was born. I wrote so many raw and honest things on the blog and have found so many amazing online friendships with readers through this story. You can learn more about our experience here.
  • Home Stuff: We bought our house in 2015 and have done multiple projects on it - from finishing the basement to adding a nursery, to creating a sunrise studio for me to do my blogging work.
  • Stage Dog: Iris is a queen and joined our family in 2015 and she’s the absolute best. She’s also the official dog mascot. You can waste your day away looking at pictures of her here.

I also share about my life on my personal Instagram @girlndogmom. I'd love if you followed me there too!
